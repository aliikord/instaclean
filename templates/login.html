InstaClean

Manage your Instagram follow requests

No data stored — everything stays in your browser
  1. Open instagram.com in Chrome and log in
  2. Press F12 to open Developer Tools
  3. Go to Application tab (top menu)
  4. In the left sidebar, click Cookies → https://www.instagram.com
  5. Find and copy these 3 values:
    • sessionid
    • ds_user_id
    • csrftoken
  6. Paste each value in the fields above
Your cookies are only stored in your browser session and are never saved to any database.
